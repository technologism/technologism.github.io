Technologism

For the consequences of technology

Why

Technology is significant and has always been. Today, in a world with more technology than ever, it is important to understand the true extent of its consequences. If not understood, one might find themselves manipulated, oppressed, exploited, or even dead while those who do understand use it to rampage across the world.

Learn more

What

The goal of this site is therefore to give a greater understanding of the consequences of technology, in order for one to better react to them once they develop in reality. One can choose to remain unaware if one is at peace with the groups who are currently getting their hands on technology, like the youth, the governments, and the rich.

Technological disruptions of the 21st century

In the pursuit of reduced suffering, humans have been using technology. Technology as a tool has no inherent aliegance, but can be used both recklessly and maliciously. Some opportunities include:

Human Obsoleteness

What will happen to humans when society no longer needs them? Machines of high intelligence and emotional understanding threaten to replace humans, not only in labor, but also in will.

Rise of Centralization

How will freedom survive the rise of oppressive regimes? New technologies allow previous flaws of oppression to be undone, all aside from the death and suffering caused by it.

Return of Conflict

New inventions allow conflict among powers to no longer result in mutually assured destruction, which is good news for some, but bad news for everyone else.

Ecological collapse

Technological accidents

With development of new technologies comes always risks. These are amplified further when the technologies in question are more powerful than ever and while desperation calls for reckless development.
