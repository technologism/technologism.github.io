Technologism

The significance of technology

Claim to relevance

Technology is significant and has always been. At this time, in a world where technology advances at a rapid accelerating pace, it is important to understand the true extent of its consequences. If not, one subjects themselves to the whims of those who do understand technology. Unlike the natives who were met by Europeans, people today have a choice.

Benefits of participation

The goal of this site is therefore to give a greater understanding of the consequences of technology, in order for one to better react once they are developed and used. By understanding this site, one understands technology and the many ways it will affect one's life, thereby more easily make decisions in mind of it.

Method of participation

The information on this site is presented through text found via clicking links. Not all text found here is equally relevant, meaning a long read is not required to extract the most important information. The most relevant part is found by scrolling down, while the less relevant parts are found in the sidebar menu.
